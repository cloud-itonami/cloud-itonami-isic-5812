# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-5812 follows open-source governance with transparent
decision-making documented in ADRs.

Scope: Back-office directory-entry/subscriber-list logging, publication
(compilation/update/print-run) scheduling, outbound distribution
coordination, and privacy/consent/accuracy concern escalation.

Out-of-scope: Directly finalizing a data-privacy-compliance decision
(GDPR/CCPA compliance determination), resolving or granting a
data-subject deletion/erasure/"right to be forgotten" request, or any
other privacy-authority determination.

See README.md for complete CRITICAL scope exclusions.
